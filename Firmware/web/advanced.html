Per-LED brightness correction (%)

Per-LED gain applied on top of the global LED brightness. 100 = no change. Lower to dim over-bright LEDs; raise to compensate for dim ones (e.g. green LEDs with the same resistor).

Index 0 is the first pin in LED_ARRAY — the physical order, not affected by the invert toggle.
